================================================================================
  TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Problem: Dataset not found
Solution: Download dataset following STEP 1 above

Problem: Training too slow
Solution: Ensure GPU is available (check with: nvidia-smi)

Problem: Out of memory
Solution: Reduce BATCH_SIZE to 32 or IMG_SIZE to 128 in script

Problem: Low accuracy
Solution: Increase EPOCHS_PHASE2 to 20 or IMG_SIZE to 224

================================================================================
  CUSTOMIZATION
================================================================================

Edit train_model_fast.py to adjust:
